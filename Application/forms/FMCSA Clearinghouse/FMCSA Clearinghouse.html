General Consent for Limited Queries of the Federa
Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA)
Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse



I , hereby provide consent to United Parcel Service (UPS) or its agent(s) to conduct a limited query of the FMCSA Commercial Driver's License Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse to determine whether drug and alcohol violation information about me exists in the Clearinghouse. This consent allows UPS to conduct multiple limited queries during the duration of my employment at UPS.

I understand that if the limited query conducted by UPS or its agent(s) indicates that drug or alcohol information about me exists in the Clearinghouse, the FMCSA will not disclose that information toUPS or its agent(s) without first obtaining additional specific consent from me.

I further understand that if I refuse to provide consent for UPS or its agent(s) to conduct a limited query of the Clearinghouse, UPS must prohibit me from performing safety-sensitive functions, including driving a commercial motor vehicle, as required by the FMCSA's drug and alcoholregulations (49 CFR Parts 382, 383,384, and 391, Docket No. FMCSA-2011-0031, RIN 2126-Ab18).
